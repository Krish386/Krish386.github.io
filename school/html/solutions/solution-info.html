Air Pollution

Planting Trees

We encourage all children to plant as many trees as possible, not just in school, but at home as well. Through activities such as providing each child with a plant, we imprint the values of nurturing a plant, through which they will not only improve the air quality in their homes, but will also learn how they can plant and nurture a plant on their own.
