ABOUT US

As a school we believe in imprinting the values of nurturing nature into every child in our school. We believe that the only way we can change how we treat nature, is by imparting those values into the future generation.

With the intent of developing a harmonious relationship between children and nature an Eco-Club has been formed comprising of students and teachers. They call themselves as the Eco Club warriors and have taken the oath to arise, awake and work together to save the mother Earth.

As the maiden initiative of the club, paper bags were given to the prefects of every class to discourage the use of plastic. Frequent awareness programs are conducted to sensitize students, parents and teachers about the need to protect our Mother Nature.

We aim towards working for a world where each one of us can call ourselves as the GREEN WARRIORS.
